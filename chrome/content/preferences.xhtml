Zometaker Auto-update, normalise, and complete metadata for items in your Zotero library. APA 7 reference formatting. Uses CrossRef / OpenAlex / DataCite APIs. CrossRef's API etiquette asks you to identify yourself via the User-Agent header. Adding a contact e-mail here is the polite thing to do (and they'll contact you if your client misbehaves). Rule ids implemented: R1 surname-comma-initials, R4 all-caps given names, R5 date / n.d., R6 article title sentence case, R7 journal title case, R8 volume numeric, R9 issue numeric, R11 en-dash pages, R12 DOI URL form, R13 ISBN 10/13, R14 publisher no location, R15 edition "2nd ed.", R17 missing required fields, R18 single-field author (D. A. Titone & Connine fix), R19 strip HTML tags & weird whitespace in fields, R20 all-caps family name.
